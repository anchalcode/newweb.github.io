About Us

'Nirvana Yoga' is giving opportunities to all, by providing services based on traditional yoga and modern science to heal, grow and develop themselves. Many online classes and live sessions are organized to aware people about yoga. Additionally therapy is provided using classical yoga. Free treatment for the impoverished is our main project on which we are working. To support this, contributions are taken from individuals who are competent. This connected them together and inspired social help.
